About Qiniu Cloud

Qiniu Cloud is affiliated to Shanghai Qiniu Information Technologies Co., Ltd. Qiniu, the leading enterprise cloud service supplier in China, specializes in the research, development and operation of cloud computing service focusing on data management. We have launched products for rich media, including object storage, integrated CDN accelerating, container-based computing cloud, big data platform and AI platform, and offered one-stop solution to video cloud as well. Our company has so far led the domestic cloud computing industry and supplied services to more than 700,000 enterprises in order to help enterprises quickly get their own clouds and create larger business value.

History of Qiniu Cloud

  • 2011
  • August, 2011

    Qiniu was founded.

  • December, 2011

    Cloud platforms went into operation online.

  • 2012
  • February, 2012

    Qiniu raised first round of capital of millions of yuan from Matrix Partners China.

  • December, 2012

    Go Programming Language, a work by Qiniu, is awarded one of the "Top 10 Books in 2012".

  • 2013
  • January, 2013

    Qiniu raised second round of capital of tens of millions USD from Qiming Venture Partners and Matrix Partners China.

  • 2014
  • July, 2014

    Qiniu launched Storage 2.0, which saved 62% of unit storage costs.

  • August, 2014

    Qiniu raised third round of capital of tens of millions USD led by CBC Capital and followed by Matrix Partners China and Qiming Venture Partners.

  • December, 2014

    Qiniu launched accelerating service abroad covering 54 areas in 31 countries across the world.

  • 2015
  • January, 2015

    The object storage service of Qiniu achieved Credible Cloud Service Award.

  • August, 2015

    Qiniu officially launched the data processing platform based on container service technology.

  • 2016
  • February, 2016

    Qiniu raised fourth round of capital from well-known investment institutions like F&G Venture, GHII, Zhangjiang High-tech and Telstra.

  • May, 2016

    Qiniu launched PILI in the mobile era.

  • December, 2016

    Qiniu was certified by the ISO/IEC 27001:2013 Information Security Management System.

  • 2017
  • January 2017

    Qiniu launched smart big data platform.

  • April 2017

    Qiniu launched fusion CDN abroad covering over 100 countries on 6 continents.

  • August 2017

    Qiniu raised fifth round of capital of 1,000 million led by Alibaba group and YunFeng capital.

  • September 2017

    Qiniu AI team won the runner-up prize in ACMMM LSVC, and video identification ability was recognized by international.

  • October 2017

    Qiniu was certified with CDN service certificate and cloud service certificate by MIIT.

  • 2018
  • January 2018

    Qiniu released its own-developed multimedia player.
