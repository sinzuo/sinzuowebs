Product Advantages

Highly Reliable

The industry-leading storage scheme with erasure codes can provide 99.99999999999999% data reliability. Replica redundancy across data centers ensures the high availability of service.

Easy to Expand

The flexible storage system increases the agility of your business.There's no need to worry about both the ceiling and the expansion of the storage space with KODO.

Low in Cost

KODO can be used and paid as needed, which brings convenience to effectively avoid the waste of unused storage and bandwidth resource.

Intelligent in Data

Closely collaborated with other Qiniu's products, KODO provides standard HDFS access method and greatly optimizes the mass high-speed reading and writing scenarios of big data and machine learning.

Core Functions and Services

Multimedia Data Processing

Based on the object storage, you can achieve one-stop image and audio/video processing.

Trans-regional Synchronization

Trans-regional synchronization provides users with high-efficient data migration and synchronization in different storage regions through easy operations to achieve remote data disaster recovery.

Life Cycle Management

Users can make rules for their storage space based on their business needs with life cycle management. The storage object will automatically conduct conversions of storage types or deletions at fixed time.

Low-frequency Storage

Low-frequency storage provides less expensive prices than standard storage and can be applied to business scenarios with real-time access to data and low RF, such as enterprises'data backups, data monitoring, applications of net disks and so on.

Mirrored Storage

Mirrored storage is applicable to migrating existing data in original business systems, which helps users complete data migrations seamlessly without affecting access to the original business systems.

Upload/ Download

Qiniu supplies users with various APIs and tools for different upload or download scenarios and supports uploads from servers and direct uploads from clients with acceleration services.

Application Scenarios

  • Private Cloud Storage
  • Multimedia Data Processing
  • Accelerated Distribution of Content
  • High Concurrency of Mass Data
  • Backup and Archive
  • Hosting of Static Resources

Enterprise clients can deploy the KODO system in private. Solutions of the private cloud storage have features of high reliability, strong safety and easy expansion based on accumulated experience from public cloud storage. Meanwhile mature management, operation and maintenance of the system are offered to enable companies to meet their requirements in capacity, performance and stability with affordable products and costs on operation and maintenance.

For scenarios of mass users'content generating, the ability of high concurrence of KODO enables you to flexibly deal with business scenarios with mass traffic. Data, such as images, audios, videos and other files stored in the cloud, can be processed.

For business scenarios of hotspot resources distributions, such as videos and games, distributions can be accelerated with KODO as the source station and FUSION in order to visit resources nearby through CDN nodes and improve user experience.

Qiniu makes a deep optimization for scenarios of high concurrency of mass data to achieve the conversion from object storage access protocols to HDFS protocols. Compared with the traditional Hadoop solution, users can get better performance in reading and analyzing with more stability and better expansibility.

Qiniu offers highly available and reliable storage solutions to backup and archive key data. Users can set different access permissions and levels to ensure the safety of data access. Compared with the traditional self-established solution, users do not need to purchase expensive hardware or worry about issues of expansion and safety in order to save more costs on storage, operation and human resources.

Qiniu supports various third-party expansion plug-ins seamlessly, such as WordPress, Discz and Emlog, and offers the hosting of static resources from various expansion plug-ins to Qiniu with one click.
